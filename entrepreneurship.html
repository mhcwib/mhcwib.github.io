Overview

The Mount Holyoke Entrepreneurship Club aims to inspire, teach, and connect Mount Holyoke women to become innovative business leaders and social entrepreneurs.

Sign up for the weekly newsletter to get tips, updates, and the scoop on any upcoming events!

What We Do

  • Stimulate leadership and entrepreneurial spirit
  • Help give Students practical skills for careers
  • Provide productive tools to create opportunities for students interested in starting their own business.
  • Establish and maintain networking relations among students, faculty, trustees, alumnae, and the surrounding community
